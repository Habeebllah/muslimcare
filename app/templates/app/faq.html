FAQs

  • Why should I trust your online donation form?

    Here’s what you should include in your answer:

    • The name of the technology platform and payment gateway that you use.
    • Your vendor’s PCI status (that’s payment card industry status for security)

    We hope your vendors are PCI Level 1 (like FrontStream!) Credit card data should always be encrypted when it’s captured online.

  • Who has access to our donor data?

    Only organization staff has access to donor information and NEVER have access to credit card numbers other than the last 4 digits for verification or update purposes.

  • I didn’t mean to donate that amount. Can I get a refund?

    Yes. Contact us within 24 hours of donation for a charge-back

  • Can I make an in-kind donation?

    Yes. Contact us for the location, days and times drop-offs

  • Can I donate to honor or remember someone?

    You can donate in honor / in memory / in celebration of someone dear to you. You can send eCards to notify a friend, family or boss that a donation has been made in their name.

  • What can I expect after I donate to your cause?

    You will be receiving a quarterly report and newsletter by mail on how your donations made a difference. You’ll be receiving event invites

  • How do I volunteer for your cause?

    Volunteers are the backbone to achieving the objectives of the organization

  • Donate services, not goods
