About us

Our Mission

The Centre is a non-governmental organization that seeks institutional transformation of the society by advancing Civil and Social Protection Rights of Muslims and non-Muslims in Nigeria, as well as to enhance the development of spiritual values as embodied in Islam. We help governments and societies build a sustainably peaceful, prosperous and just world.  This we do by fostering mutual understanding between interest groups through debate, dialogue and independent analysis and promoting excellence in the principles and practice of:

CivicParticipation

We build capacities for conflict resolution, civic education and strengthening the rule of law as a panacea for building trust, cooperation and accountability in governance.


Social Protection

We seek to reduce poverty and unemployment by advocating and promoting policies, skills and resources that will facilitate a thriving culture of entrepreneurship and wealth creation among Women, Youth and other vulnerable groups.


Leadership

The realities of an ever-changing world demands leadership skills and knowledge that is humane, innovative and responsive to new threats and opportunities. We bridge the skills and knowledge gap in government, politics, religion, sports, entertainment and the corporate world for greater inclusion and sustainable development.
